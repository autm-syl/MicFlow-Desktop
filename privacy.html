Privacy Policy

How MicFlow handles your data and privacy.

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the MicFlow mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

MicFlow turns your phone into a wireless microphone and speaker that streams audio to and from a paired PC over your local Wi‑Fi network.

Information We Collect

MicFlow does not require an account and we do not ask you for your name, email, or other personal identifiers to use the core features.

We, as the developer, do not collect or store your audio or the contents of your streams on any server. However, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may automatically collect limited technical data as described in the "Advertising" section below.

Audio & Local Network

  • Microphone: When you start sending, the App accesses your microphone to stream audio to your PC. Audio is transmitted directly to the device you choose on your local network and is not recorded or stored by us.
  • Local network: The App uses your local Wi‑Fi network to discover and connect to your PC (via UDP). This traffic stays within your own network and is not routed through our servers.
  • IP addresses: Device IP addresses on your local network are used only to establish the connection and (optionally) for your own IP‑filter settings stored on your device. They are not transmitted to us.

Advertising

MicFlow displays banner ads through Google AdMob. We have configured AdMob to serve non‑personalized ads. To deliver and measure ads, Google may process limited technical information such as device type, coarse location (country/region), and ad interaction data, in accordance with Google's policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses data from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Depending on your region, the App may present a consent choice (Google's User Messaging Platform) before ads are shown.

Issue Reports

If you choose to use the in‑app "Report an Issue" feature, the message you write — along with an optional name you provide and basic device information (device name and iOS version) — is sent to the developer so we can diagnose the problem. This is entirely voluntary; if you do not submit a report, none of this information is sent.

Children's Privacy

MicFlow is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Your Choices

  • You can revoke microphone access at any time in your device's Settings ▸ Privacy ▸ Microphone.
  • Where applicable, you can change your advertising consent choices through the in‑app privacy options, or manage tracking settings on your device.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored settings from your device.
