Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 05, 2026

MicFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application and desktop software.

1. Data Collection

Audio Data: MicFlow captures audio via your device's microphone or system output for the sole purpose of streaming it to your connected device. We do not record, store, or upload your audio data to any external servers. All audio transmission occurs directly over your local Wi-Fi network.

Personal Information: We do not require account registration. We do not collect personal identifiers such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. Device Permissions

To function correctly, MicFlow requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone: To capture your voice for the Wireless Mic feature.
  • Local Network: To discover and connect to your PC/Mobile device.

3. Security

Since data remains within your private local network, the security of your audio stream depends on your Wi-Fi network's security settings. We recommend using a password-protected WPA2/WPA3 network.

4. Third-Party Services

MicFlow does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.
