MicFlow

The ultimate bridge for high-fidelity audio between your PC and Mobile device over Wi-Fi.

Download for Windows (.exe)

1. Wireless PC Microphone

Turn your smartphone into a high-fidelity wireless microphone for your computer.

MicFlow captures your voice using your mobile device’s built-in hardware and streams it instantly to your PC over Wi-Fi. By integrating with a virtual audio cable, it acts as a native system input—perfect for crystal-clear communication in Discord, Zoom, or OBS.

2. Remote PC Speaker

Stream your computer’s system audio directly to your mobile device.

Listen to your PC’s music, movies, or game audio through your phone’s speakers or connected headphones. MicFlow broadcasts high-definition audio with ultra-low latency, turning your mobile device into a long-range wireless headset.
