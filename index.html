1. Wireless PC Microphone

Turn your smartphone into a high-fidelity wireless microphone for your computer.

MicFlow captures your voice using your mobile device's built-in hardware and streams it instantly to your PC over Wi-Fi. With optional virtual audio cable routing, it can act as a system input for Discord, Zoom, OBS, and other apps.

2. Remote PC Speaker

Stream your computer's system audio directly to your mobile device.

Listen to your PC's music, movies, or game audio through your phone's speakers or connected headphones. MicFlow broadcasts high-definition audio with low latency over your local network.
